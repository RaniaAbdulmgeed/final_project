Store Policy

1. Return & Exchange Policy

You can return or exchange products within 14 days of receiving your order, provided they are in their original condition.

2. Shipping & Delivery

Orders are processed within 2-4 business days, and delivery takes 3-7 business days depending on the destination.

3. Payment Policy

We offer multiple payment methods, including cash on delivery and credit/debit card payments.

4. Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your personal data and will not share it with third parties.

5. Promotions & Discounts

Offers and discounts are available for a limited time and may be changed or canceled by management at any time.
